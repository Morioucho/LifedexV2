Lifedex is a simple website coded by 4 High School students. This is a simple web application that intends to help users who are new to living by themselves. Guides on various amounts of topics are provided. There are "How To" tutorials on how to do basic tasks such as "How to fold your laundry" or "How to cook food". Easy recipes to cook food such as Avacado on Toast. Tips and Tricks as well as Financial Advice.
