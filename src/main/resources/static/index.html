Lifedex, A Home For Everyone

A website full of tutorials on lifeskills for people who are beginning to live on their own!

Recipes

For most people starting to live by themselves, these are some easy recipes to read. Some examples are "How to make Avocado on Toast", "How to make a Grilled Cheese Sandwich", or even "How to make Bacon and Eggs"!

Life Skills

Most people may need refreshers on what they have learned before living by themselves. For example, some people need to remember how to clean their kitchen, fold their clothes, or make their bed in the most efficient way possible.

Tutorials

We have easy to follow tutorials on a variety of topics. Some may include how to change their oil on their car, how to open a bank account, or even how to develop social skills.
