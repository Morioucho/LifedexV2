LifeDex is a simple website coded by four high school students. This is a simple web application that intends to help users who are new to living by themselves through guides on various types of topics. LifeDex provides "How To" tutorials on how to do basic tasks such as "How to fold your laundry," easy recipes to cook such as avocado on toast, and tips and tricks such as financial advice.
